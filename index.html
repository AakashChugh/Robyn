Robyn

Marketing Mix Models for all

Get Started

Take control

Robyn was designed from the ground up to empower businesses like yours. Its design enables you to take control of your marketing mix decisions by means of models that you own and can update in time.

Focus on What Matters

Robyn lets you focus on the important aspects of Marketing Mix models such as the business contextualization of variables. Instead of losing time, let Robyn do the initial work and keep adjusting media parameters according to your experience.

Calibrate your model

Robyn includes features that allow you to Calibrate your model using information from conversion lift and geo lift studies for any publisher.
